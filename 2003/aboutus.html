About Us

OH NO! News is a locally-owned and operated news station serving the community since 1995. Founded by Margaret Mellow, our goal is to provide the most accurate and up-to-date news to our viewers.

Meet the Team

  • Margaret Mellow

    As the founder and CEO of OH NO! News, Margaret has been the driving force behind the station's success. With over 20 years of experience in the industry, she is a respected voice in the community and a tireless advocate for journalistic integrity.

  • Ronald McDonald Sr.

    Ronald is the chief news anchor for OH NO! News, bringing his signature charm and wit to every broadcast. With over 15 years of experience in the field, he is a trusted voice in the community and a beloved figure among viewers.
